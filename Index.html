Understanding Banking Transactions

P2P (Peer to Peer)

This is a system where individuals can send and receive money directly without an intermediary. It is most commonly seen in payment apps like PayPal, Venmo, and Zelle.

B2C (Business to Consumer)

B2C transactions are between businesses and individual consumers. This includes things like purchasing items online, paying for services, or subscription-based models like Netflix or Spotify.

C2B (Consumer to Business)

A C2B transaction occurs when a consumer provides goods or services to a business. For example, a freelance worker providing a service to a company, or an individual selling products to a business.

B2B (Business to Business)

This type of transaction happens between two businesses. Examples include one company selling supplies to another or companies collaborating on joint ventures or shared services.
